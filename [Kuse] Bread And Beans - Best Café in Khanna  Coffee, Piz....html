Our Story

A cozy corner where every cup tells a story

Welcome to Bread And Beans

Nestled on the first floor of Arora Bakery on Grand Trunk Road, Bread And Beans is Khanna's beloved café destination. We've created a warm, inviting space where friends gather, couples connect, and food lovers discover their new favorites.

Our café combines the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee with the comfort of a home away from home. From our signature pizzas to indulgent desserts, every dish is crafted with care and passion.

With cozy seating, warm lighting, and an aesthetic that's perfect for your Instagram feed, we're more than just a café – we're your daily escape into comfort and flavor.
